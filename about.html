About Us

Welcome to Bloomade, your go-to destination for beautiful bouquets of flowers. We are a new company passionate about bringing smiles to our customers with our carefully curated floral arrangements. Our story began with four friends, each with a unique love for flowers and a shared dream of spreading joy through nature's finest creations.

Meet Our Founders

ANIS ALISA: The artistic soul who breathes life into each arrangement with her creative touch.

FARAHAFIQAH: The nature enthusiast with an eye for selecting the freshest and most vibrant blooms.

HANNAH BASYIRAH: The visionary mind behind Bloomade's brand and customer experience.

ASHRIN SOFIA: The heart of the team, ensuring every bouquet is crafted and delivered with love and care.

Together, we embarked on this floral journey to celebrate life's moments - big or small - with the language of flowers. Our aim is to create unforgettable experiences, filling each petal with the emotions that words often fail to express.

Thank you for choosing Bloomade, and allowing us to be a part of your special moments.
